## 0. TL;DR · 핵심 요약

**EN —** The space is more crowded than the first PRD draft assumed, and two findings reshape our positioning:
1. **Polarsteps already ships "Trip Reels" (Summer 2025)** — auto map‑animated reels from your trip photos, one button, share to IG/TikTok. With **5M+ users** and top App‑Store ranking, this is our **most direct threat** — they do our *core* idea at scale.
2. **"Collect a place" is its own category** — passport‑stamp apps (Been, Stamps, PASSPRT, Stampie) already let people collect uniquely‑designed stamps per place and share a passport. So our **collection hook is not novel on its own.**

**The honest wedge:** *no one fuses all four* — (a) an auto **map‑animated reel**, (b) an **in‑reel collectible sticker** mechanic, (c) **trip‑type mood + production aesthetic** (polaroids, Journey Card, transport modes incl. flight), (d) **KR‑first + gifting**. Each piece exists somewhere; **the combination is the moat — but it must be executed sharply and fast, because Polarsteps is one feature‑release away from our lane.**

**KR —** 시장은 1차 PRD 가정보다 붐빈다. 두 가지가 포지셔닝을 바꾼다:
1. **Polarsteps가 이미 "Trip Reels"(2025 여름)를 출시** — 여행 사진을 자동으로 지도 애니메이션 릴스로, 원버튼, IG/TikTok 공유. **사용자 5백만+** & 앱스토어 상위 = **가장 직접적인 위협**(우리 *코어*를 규모로 하고 있음).
2. **"장소 수집"은 이미 하나의 카테고리** — 여권 스탬프 앱(Been·Stamps·PASSPRT·Stampie)이 장소별 고유 스탬프를 수집·공유하게 한다. **수집 훅 자체는 새롭지 않다.**

**정직한 쐐기:** *이 넷을 동시에 융합한 곳은 없다* — (a) 자동 **지도 애니메이션 릴스**, (b) **릴스 안의 수집 스티커** 메커닉, (c) **여행 종류별 무드 + 프로덕션 미감**(폴라로이드·여행 카드·비행 포함 이동수단), (d) **한국 시장 우선 + 선물**. 각 조각은 어딘가 존재하지만 **조합이 해자다 — 단, 날카롭고 빠르게 실행해야 한다. Polarsteps는 피처 한 번이면 우리 레인에 들어온다.**

## 2. Deep‑Dives · 상세 분석

### 2.1 Polarsteps — ⚠️ #1 direct threat · 직접 위협 1순위
- **Scale 규모:** 5M+ users; Top‑3 Travel App, App Store 2026.
- **What 무엇:** auto GPS trip log → map + photos/notes → **Trip Reels (Summer 2025)**: auto‑selects best media (balances photo/video/orientation), **map scenes**, 60s auto (editable to 3:15), one‑button share. Needs ≥10 photos. AI itinerary planner. Destination guides.
- **Money 수익:** app is **free**; monetizes via **printed Travel Books €36–150** (auto‑generated, free shipping).
- **Strengths 강점:** scale, brand, network/social graph, the print‑book revenue (gifting/souvenir), the reel is *good enough* and free.
- **Weaknesses 약점:** Trip Reels map scenes **iOS‑only** (Android "coming"); reels are clean‑but‑generic (no **trip‑type mood themes**, no **polaroid/Journey‑Card** look, no transport‑mode storytelling like a **flight arc**); **no in‑reel collection/gamification**; not KR‑culture‑tuned.
- **How Stroll wins 우리가 이기는 법:** out‑*aesthetic* and out‑*game* them — trip‑type production value + the collectible Journey Card + richer mode storytelling + KR‑first + gifting. **Speed matters: assume they'll keep improving reels.**

### 2.2 Relive — sport‑only, premium‑priced · 운동 전용·고가
- **What:** 3D flyover video of a hike/ride/run from GPS; photo pop‑ups along the route; metrics.
- **Money:** Free = **1 video per activity, no edit**; **Plus ~$12–20/mo or ~$74/yr**; music & customization now paywalled.
- **vs Stroll:** Relive is locked to *outdoor sport*. Stroll covers **any** trip type (city/gallery/beach/overseas), is cheaper, and has stops + collection + a warmer aesthetic. Relive validates that **"animate my route as a video" has real demand and willingness to pay** — but in a narrow lane we sit outside.

### 2.3 Passport‑stamp apps (Been · Stamps · PASSPRT · Stampie) — they own "collection" · 수집을 선점
- **What:** collect a stamp/sticker per country/city/place; build a shareable passport/map; **PASSPRT** = GPS‑verified stamps + **uniquely designed stamp art per place** + anti‑cheat + "discover places worth collecting."
- **Money:** mostly free + one‑time premium unlock (Been: regions/globe/wishlist) or light IAP.
- **vs Stroll:** This is the uncomfortable one — **our "collect a sticker per place" mechanic already exists as a whole category.** BUT theirs is a **static passport/map**, not a **share‑worthy animated reel of the day**. Stroll's edge = the collection is **earned inside a beautiful reel of an actual day**, not a checkbox map. Still, we must **not pitch "collection" as novel** — pitch the *fusion*.

### 2.4 Google Photos / Apple Photos Memories — the free default · 무료 기본값
- **What:** auto montage of your library; Google = cinematic effects + video‑forward "highlight videos"; Apple = **AI text‑prompt Memory Movies** (Apple Intelligence) with curated music.
- **Money:** free (bundled with the OS/photo storage).
- **vs Stroll:** the gravitational floor — billions of users, zero effort, free. **No map journey, no trip‑type mood, no collection, no route/transport story.** Stroll must be visibly, obviously *more* (a map of your day you'd actually post) to justify leaving the free default.

### 2.5 디로그 (D·LOG) — the KR AI‑diary to watch · 주목할 한국 경쟁자
- **What:** Google Play **2025 Best Self‑Improvement App (KR)**; pick photos → **AI auto‑organizes time/location/weather/route into a diary**.
- **vs Stroll:** strong KR auto‑curation/diary, but **a diary, not a map‑animated share reel + collection.** Confirms KR demand for "AI does the organizing" — and that **no KR app yet does our mood‑reel + collection combo.** (KR map‑record apps 포토로그·어디 = static map logs, no reel.)

## 4. Re‑positioning (what this changes in the PRD) · PRD 수정점

1. **Drop "collection is novel."** Reframe: *"the first to fuse a share‑worthy map reel WITH an earned, in‑reel collection."* — 수집 단독이 아니라 **릴스 + 수집 융합**으로.
2. **Name Polarsteps as the benchmark, not Google Photos.** Our real fight is the *good free reel*, not the generic montage. — 진짜 경쟁은 Polarsteps Trip Reels.
3. **Lead the wedge with AESTHETIC + KR + GIFTING,** the three things incumbents are weakest on. — 미감·한국·선물로 치고 나간다.
4. **Speed is strategic.** Polarsteps is iterating reels; assume 6–12 months before they could add themes/collection. Ship the differentiated aesthetic + collection loop *before* that. — 속도가 전략. 6~12개월 안에 차별점을 박아야.

## 5. Strategic Implications · 전략적 함의

- **Don't fight the free default head‑on.** Be the *upgrade* people pay/switch for when a trip mattered. — 무료 기본값과 정면승부 X, "특별한 여행"의 업그레이드로.
- **Win on production value + collection feel** — the two things screenshots sell instantly (and what the UX expert reacted to). — 스샷이 파는 두 가지: 완성도 + 수집감.
- **KR‑first beachhead** — 포토로그/어디/디로그 prove KR loves travel‑record + AI‑curation, but none make a *postable mood reel + collection*. Own that gap in Korean first. — 한국 먼저.
- **Gifting as the growth loop** (Polarsteps monetizes print books — gifting demand is proven). Every gifted reel = a new‑user invite. — 선물 = 성장 루프(인쇄책으로 증명된 수요).
- **B2B/creator as margin** (tourism, hotels) where consumer WTP is thin. — 소비자 지갑이 얇은 곳은 B2B로.
