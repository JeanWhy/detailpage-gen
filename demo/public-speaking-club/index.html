Sharna
Founder · The Public Speaking Club

I’m a natural chatterbox, a daily SPF proponent and a wannabe runner. I’ve spent a lot of time improving my own public speaking — so I know how stressful preparing a presentation can be, and how insecure those pesky filler words make you feel.
