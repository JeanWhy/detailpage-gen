No surprises

The questions everyone asks.

Does it hurt?

Most clients describe a warming sensation with brief moments of heat. Sofwave's integrated cooling keeps the surface comfortable, and your therapist adjusts to you throughout.

Is there really no downtime?

Yes. Mild redness or slight puffiness at most, and no marks to hide. Most people return to their day immediately.

When will I see results?

Some firmness appears in the first weeks, but the real result builds as new collagen matures — typically over about three months after a single session.

How long does it last?

Results from new collagen are long-lasting, though skin keeps ageing naturally. Many choose a maintenance session later. We'll give you an honest, personalised view at your consult.

Is it safe for my skin tone?

Yes. Sofwave's ultrasound works independently of melanin, so it's suitable for all skin tones, year-round.
