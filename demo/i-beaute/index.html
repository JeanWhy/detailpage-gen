No surprises

The questions everyone asks.

Does it hurt?

Most clients describe a warming sensation with brief moments of heat as the energy is delivered. Sofwave's integrated cooling keeps the skin surface comfortable, and your therapist adjusts to you throughout.

Is there really no downtime?

Correct. You may have mild redness or slight puffiness for a short time, but there are no marks to hide. Most people return to work or their day immediately.

When will I see results?

Some firmness appears in the first weeks, but the real result builds as new collagen matures — typically over about three months after a single session.

How long does it last?

Results from new collagen are long-lasting, though your skin keeps ageing naturally. Many clients choose a maintenance session down the track. We'll give you an honest, personalised view at your consult.

Is it safe for my skin tone?

Yes. Sofwave's ultrasound energy works independently of melanin, so it's suitable for all skin tones, and can be done year-round.

How do I start?

With a complimentary consultation. We assess your skin, answer everything, and only recommend Sofwave if it's genuinely right for you. Book in seconds on Fresha.
